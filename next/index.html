How I Think

Principles that guide my work

  • Clarity before scale. Know where complexity belongs — and where it does not.
  • Agency over agents. Add AI capabilities without losing the ability for humans to understand, intervene, and remain accountable.
  • Concepts over technologies. Technologies come and go. The underlying concepts stay.
  • Open source by default. Reduce dependencies. Build in the open. On-ramp others into the community.
  • Ship, then improve. Consistency and iteration compound. Perfect is the enemy of done.
