This is a personal website. Here's what you should know about your data.

## Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how people find and use it. That means Google collects standard usage data like pages visited, time on site, and general location. I use this to know what's working and what isn't. I don't tie it to your identity.

## Theme Preference

If you toggle between dark and light mode, your preference is saved in your browser's localStorage. This stays on your device and is never sent anywhere.

## Contact Form

The contact form is powered by [Formspree](https://formspree.io). When you submit a message, your name, email, and message are sent through their service and forwarded to me. I don't store submissions beyond my inbox. Formspree has their own [privacy policy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/).

## Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to distinguish users and track sessions. I don't set any other cookies. There are no ads, no retargeting, no third-party trackers beyond GA.

## Your Data

I don't sell your data. I don't do anything weird with it. If you want something removed, [reach out](/contact/) and I'll take care of it.
